Our Beginning

From a Single Sewing Machine

StitchCraft was founded in 2005 by master tailor Rajan Pillai in a modest shop in T. Nagar, Chennai. Armed with inherited skills from three generations of tailors and an unwavering commitment to quality, Rajan began with a single sewing machine and a dream.

Word spread quickly. Clients came for alterations and stayed for custom creations. The shop grew, the team expanded, and by 2012 we had opened our second location. Today, StitchCraft employs 12 skilled artisans and serves thousands of clients annually.

We remain family-run, deeply rooted in our community, and passionate about the transformative power of a perfectly fitted garment.
